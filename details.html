Donkey Kong

Donkey Kong is the star of Nintendo's long-running Donkey Kong series, where he embarks on adventures to protect his banana hoard and home on Donkey Kong Island. He is a powerful and friendly gorilla known for his immense strength, laid-back personality, and iconic red necktie. Since his debut in the original Donkey Kong arcade game in 1981, he has become one of Nintendo's most recognizable characters. In the Super Smash Bros. series, Donkey Kong is a heavyweight fighter who uses powerful punches, grabs, and his signature Ground Pound to overwhelm opponents.
